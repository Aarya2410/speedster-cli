Measure Your Internet Speed from the Command Line

Speedster is a lightweight and powerful CLI tool that provides accurate and detailed internet speed metrics, helping you diagnose connection issues and optimize your network performance.

$ curl -sSL https://aarya2410.github.io/speedster-cli/install.sh | sh
Learn More

Key Features

Comprehensive Speed Tests

Get detailed metrics for ping, download, and upload speeds to fully understand your connection's performance.

Real-time Results

Watch the progress of your speed test in real-time with elegant spinners and immediate feedback.

CLI First

Designed for developers and power users who live in the terminal. No need to open a browser.

Why Choose Speedster?

Lightning Fast

Built with performance in mind, Speedster gives you quick and accurate results every time.

Pinpoint Accuracy

Uses a reliable testing methodology to ensure the speed metrics are as accurate as possible.

Lightweight

No bloated features. Just a simple, fast, and effective tool for checking your internet speed.

Open Source

Speedster is free and open-source. Contributions and feedback are always welcome!
